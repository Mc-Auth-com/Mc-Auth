What is Mc-Auth.com?

Mc-Auth.com allows users to login with their minecraft account without requiring the account password.
Other websites and developers can then request verification that you have access to that minecraft account.

Thanks to Mc-Auth.com, all kinds of websites can provide a secure way of a 'Minecraft-Login' feature.

How does it work?

You are a developer? Have a look at the documentation.

  1. Visit ${URL_BASE}/login
  2. Enter your Minecraft-Username (or UUID if you are one of those crazy persons)
  3. Start Minecraft and try connecting to ${MINECRAFT_HOST}
  4. Enter the 6-digit code you see in Minecraft, to complete your login

  5. Confirm or Dismiss the shown message to verify your account
